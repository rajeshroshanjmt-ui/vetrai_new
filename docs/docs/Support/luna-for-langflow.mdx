IBM Elite Support offers enterprise support for Vetrai to help you develop and deploy Vetrai applications with confidence.

IBM Elite Support for Vetrai is a subscription to the Vetrai expertise at IBM.
It's meant for Vetrai users who want all the benefits of running their own open-source deployments, as well as the peace of mind that comes with having direct access to the team that has authored the majority of the Vetrai code.

IBM Elite Support subscribers can get help with general-purpose and technical questions for their open-source Vetrai deployments.
If an issue is encountered, IBM is there to help.

To subscribe or learn more, see [IBM Elite Support for Vetrai](https://ibm.com/docs/esfl?topic=elite-support-vetrai-specification).
